# Multi-Person Analysis Methodology (多命合参)

Analyze multiple charts together for family, couples, business partnerships.

## Analysis Order

1. Analyze each person INDIVIDUALLY first (complete individual analysis)
2. Identify the "lead chart" - usually the person asking, or the breadwinner
3. Compare patterns and Five Element relationships
4. Analyze interactions between charts
5. Identify complementary strengths and conflict points
6. Provide combined feng shui and timing recommendations

## Five Element Interactions Between People

**Generating cycle (相生):**
- Person A's element generates Person B's element = A supports B naturally
- In family: natural caregiver/energy source relationship
- In couple: natural support dynamic

**Overcoming cycle (相克):**
- Person A's element overcomes Person B's element = A controls/constrains B
- In family: can be healthy guidance or excessive control depending on balance
- In couple: can create dynamic tension or productive challenge

**Same element:**
- Strong bond but potential competition
- Need clear role differentiation to avoid rivalry

**Practical examples:**
- Fire (命二) generates Earth (命一) generates Metal (命三) = natural family chain
- Male DM overcomes Female DM element = traditional husband-leads dynamic
- Female DM overcomes Male DM element = wife has natural influence over husband

## Couple Analysis (合婚)

### Step 1: Spouse Star Assessment
- Male chart: Check wealth stars (正财/偏财) quality and position
- Female chart: Check authority stars (正官/七杀) quality and position
- Missing or heavily attacked spouse star = relationship challenges

### Step 2: Marriage Palace (夫妻宫) Assessment
- Day branch (日支) = marriage palace
- Check for clashes (冲), penalties (刑), harm (害) from other pillars
- Clash on marriage palace = relationship instability

### Step 3: Pattern Harmony
- Complementary patterns create attraction and growth
- Same pattern can create understanding but also competition
- Opposing patterns (e.g., Output + Authority) create friction but also dynamic balance

### Step 4: Combined Luck Timing
- Check if good/bad luck periods align or alternate
- Couples with alternating luck (one up while one down) can support each other
- Couples with simultaneous bad luck need extra caution

### Step 5: Children Analysis
- Male: Seven Killings and Proper Authority = children
- Female: Food God and Output = children
- Children palace (时柱) indicates relationship with children

## Family Analysis (家宅)

### Three-Chart Minimum
For family of three (father/mother/child), analyze:
1. Individual destinies
2. Parent-child element interactions
3. Sibling interactions (if multiple children)
4. Family energy flow (谁生谁, 谁克谁)

### Family Dynamics
- **Energy source:** Who's element generates others? This person naturally energizes the family.
- **Stabilizer:** Who's element is earth or central? This person grounds the family.
- **Challenger:** Who overcomes others? This person drives change and growth.

### 🆕 Cross-Generational Lineage Analysis (跨代际传承分析)

> 当分析2代以上家族成员时，需要超越标准多命合参（同代际），进入**跨代际的家族命理基因分析**。

See bazi-master-agent's `references/family-lineage-analysis.md` for the full methodology. Key differences from standard multi-life analysis:

| 维度 | 标准多命合参 | 跨代际传承分析 |
|:----|:-----------|:--------------|
| **时间跨度** | 同代或相邻 | 跨2-3代 |
| **核心问题** | 谁克谁？谁生谁？ | 什么"基因"在代际间传递？ |
| **关键指标** | 互补率、合冲 | 共享地支、五行极值演化、格局演变 |
| **分析目标** | 家庭和谐建议 | 解释第三代罕见格局的成因 |

**核心发现模式**：当某五行在G1旺、G2弱后，G3很可能出现该五行的"反弹式"极致化。第三代的罕见格局往往是前两代能量累积到临界点后的命理"显化"。

### Combined Feng Shui
- **House orientation:** Match to the weakest element in the family
- **Bedroom allocation:** Each person's bedroom in their favorable direction
- **Common areas:** Colors and decorations balancing the family's overall Five Elements
- **Study area:** Enhance Resource stars (印) for academic performance

## Business Partnership Analysis

### Element Compatibility
- **Best:** One generates the other (相生) = natural support
- **Good:** Same element with clear role division
- **Risky:** One overcomes the other (相克) = potential conflict unless balanced
- **Dangerous:** Clashing patterns with no harmony mechanism

### Pattern Compatibility
- **Output + Wealth:** Innovator + Financier
- **Authority + Resource:** Manager + Advisor
- **Sibling + Wealth:** Team player + Revenue generator
- **Avoid:** Two Output patterns (both want to lead differently) or two Authority patterns (power struggle)

### Partnership Timing
- Check if partners enter compatible luck periods simultaneously
- Partnership starts in favorable year for BOTH parties
- Contract year should avoid clash years for either party

1. Identify which person's useful god dominates the year

3. Other family members support or adjust
4. Avoid years when multiple family members have taboo elements active
