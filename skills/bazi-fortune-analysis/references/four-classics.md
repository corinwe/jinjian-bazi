# Four Classics Synthesis Methodology (四书合参)

When analyzing any birth chart, apply all four classics. Each gives a unique lens. Synthesize for final verdict.

## 《渊海子平》- Yuan Hai Zi Ping (Pattern Classic)

**Focus:** Pattern identification from month command (月令定格).

**Method:**
1. Identify the dominant Ten God in the month branch (monthly decree)
2. Check if the pattern is pure (clear) or mixed (杂)
3. Identify the useful god (用神) based on pattern
4. Assess pattern completeness (格局成败)

**Key Principle:** "先看月令定格，再看配合" - First look at month branch to set pattern, then examine coordination.

**When to prioritize:** For pattern identification, career direction, and general life framework.

**Common patterns and useful gods:**
- 伤官格 (Output): Useful god is Wealth (财) or Authority (官). If weak body, Resource (印) becomes useful.
- 建禄格 (Jian Lu): Useful god is Wealth (财) or Authority (官/杀). Output can also help.
- 七杀格 (Seven Killings): Useful god is Food God (食神制杀) or Resource (印绶化杀).

## 《三命通会》- San Ming Tong Hui (Comprehensive Classic)

**Focus:** Detailed Ten God interactions, annual predictions, and life events.

**Method:**
1. Examine all Ten God combinations and their effects
2. Check for special structures (特殊格局)
3. Analyze stem-branch interactions (刑冲合害)
4. Cross-reference with annual/decade luck

**Key Principle:** "神煞为辅，十神为主" - Auxiliary gods supplement, but Ten Gods are primary.

**When to prioritize:** For detailed year-by-year predictions, marriage timing, wealth timing, and specific event forecasting.

**Important combinations to check:**
- 伤官见官 (Output meets Authority): Dangerous for career and health
- 财星坏印 (Wealth destroys Resource): Bad for studies and mother's health
- 食神制杀 (Food God controls Seven Killings): Noble pattern, success through talent
- 杀印相生 (Seven Killings and Resource mutually generate): Power and wisdom combined

## 《滴天髓》- Di Tian Sui (Nature Classic)

**Focus:** Five element nature, weather/temperature (调候), and body-material balance (体用).

**Method:**
1. Determine element nature of day master (what it is and what it needs)
2. Assess temperature adjustment (调候用神) - which element balances climate
3. Check body-material (体用) relationship
4. Evaluate overall chart balance (中和)

**Key Principle:** "能知衰旺之真机，其于三命之奥，思过半矣" - Understanding true rise/fall is half the mastery.

**When to prioritize:** For body strength determination, health analysis, and understanding the innate nature of the person.

**Temperature adjustment:**
- Born in summer (巳午未 months): needs Water (水) to cool
- Born in winter (亥子丑 months): needs Fire (火) to warm
- Born in spring (寅卯辰 months): needs Fire for warmth, beware of excessive Wood
- Born in autumn (申酉戌 months): needs Fire for warmth, beware of excessive Metal

## 《子平真诠》- Zi Ping Zhen Quan (True Interpretation)

**Focus:** Pattern purity, useful god selection, and chart layers (格局高低).

**Method:**
1. Determine pattern from month branch hidden stem
2. Check if pattern is pure (one clear Ten God) or mixed
3. Select useful god based on pattern needs
4. Assess chart quality by pattern completeness and useful god protection

**Key Principle:** "八字用神，专求月令" - Useful god is primarily sought in the month branch.

**When to prioritize:** For final useful god determination, chart quality assessment, and refined career direction.

## Synthesis Protocol (合参流程)

1. Start with 《子平真诠》for initial pattern identification
2. Use 《渊海子平》to confirm and name the pattern
3. Apply 《滴天髓》for body strength and temperature adjustment
4. Use 《三命通会》for detailed Ten God interactions and predictions
5. If classics conflict:
   - Body strength conflicts: 《滴天髓》takes priority for nature/essence
   - Pattern conflicts: 《子平真诠》takes priority for structure
   - Event predictions: 《三命通会》takes priority for specifics
   - Career/life framework: 《渊海子平》takes priority for broad direction

6. Final verdict must be consistent across ALL FOUR or explain why one classic overrides

## User Fact Integration (事实反馈融合)

When user provides life facts, they OVERRIDE classical predictions:
- If user says career was bad in a "good" year → body strength was misjudged, re-evaluate
- If user says marriage is harmonious when chart predicts conflict → check for hidden harmony mechanisms
- If user says wealth came from unexpected source → check alternative wealth paths

**Golden Rule:** The classics are theoretical frameworks. User's life facts are ground truth. Always adjust theory to fit facts, never force facts into theory.
