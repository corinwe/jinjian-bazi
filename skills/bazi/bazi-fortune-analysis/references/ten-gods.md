# Ten Gods Reference (十神详解)

Ten Gods are derived from the relationship between other stems and the Day Master (日元). They represent different life domains and psychological traits.

## Generation Rules

For Day Master (DM) of element E and polarity P (Yang/Yin):

| Element | Same polarity | Opposite polarity |
|---------|-------------|-------------------|
| Same element | 比肩 (Sibling) | 劫财 (Robbery) |
| Birth element | 偏印 (偏印) | 正印 (正印) |
| Born element | 食神 (Food God) | 伤官 (Output) |
| Overcome element | 偏财 (偏财) | 正财 (正财) |
| Overcome by | 七杀 (Seven Killings) | 正官 (正官) |

## Detailed Meanings

### 正印 (Resource) - Mother, Knowledge, Protection
- **Positive:** Academic excellence, wisdom, noble-minded, patient, blessed with mentors
- **Negative:** Lazy, dependent, overthinking, conservative
- **Career:** Academic research, education, culture, religion, consulting
- **Health:** Digestive system (spleen/stomach), immunity
- **Body weak + Resource as useful god:** Must protect Resource. Wealth destroying Resource = disaster for studies and health.

### 偏印 (Partial Resource) - Unconventional Knowledge, Intuition
- **Positive:** Creative thinking, unique perspective, technical talent, spiritual sensitivity
- **Negative:** Eccentric, stubborn, loner, overthinking
- **Career:** Alternative medicine, esoteric fields, innovative technology, art
- **When dominant:** Person thinks differently from mainstream, often attracted to unconventional paths

### 正官 (Proper Authority) - Career, Status, Discipline
- **Positive:** Responsible, law-abiding, respected, good at management, stable career
- **Negative:** Rigid, conformist, afraid of risk, overly cautious
- **Career:** Government, large corporations, management, law, civil service
- **For females:** Husband star (夫星). Quality of Zheng Guan indicates husband quality.

### 七杀 (Seven Killings) - Pressure, Challenge, Power
- **Positive:** Ambitious, decisive, strong leadership, courageous
- **Negative:** Aggressive, risky, prone to conflict, excessive pressure
- **Career:** Military, police, entrepreneurship, high-pressure leadership roles
- **Control mechanisms:** Must be controlled by Food God (食神制杀) or transformed by Resource (印绶化杀)
- **Uncontrolled:** Disaster pattern - accidents, lawsuits, violence, health crises

### 正财 (Proper Wealth) - Stable Income, Wife
- **Positive:** Financially responsible, good at saving, stable income, honest
- **Negative:** Stingy, materialistic, risk-averse in finance
- **For males:** Primary wife star (妻星)
- **Wealth analysis:** DM must be strong enough to hold Zheng Cai. Weak DM + strong Cai = wealth brings disaster.

### 偏财 (Partial Wealth) - Variable Income, Business, Father
- **Positive:** Business acumen, investment talent, generous, opportunistic wealth
- **Negative:** Gambling tendency, unstable income, financial risk
- **For males:** Secondary wife/girlfriend indicator (if multiple)
- **For all:** Father star (父星) - Pian Cai represents father
- **Wealth analysis:** Business and investment earnings. Requires DM strength or Sibling support.

### 食神 (Food God) - Expression, Creativity, Enjoyment
- **Positive:** Artistic, eloquent, optimistic, good food/life appreciation, gentle
- **Negative:** Overindulgence, lazy pursuit of pleasure, unrealistic
- **Career:** Arts, food industry, entertainment, teaching, creative fields
- **Key function:** Controls Seven Killings (制杀). Food God controlling Killings = powerful noble pattern.

### 伤官 (Output) - Innovation, Rebellion, Intelligence
- **Positive:** Brilliant, creative, expressive, innovative, independent thinker
- **Negative:** Rebellious, critical of authority, blunt, relationship problems
- **Career:** Entrepreneurship, R&D, consulting, arts, any field requiring innovation
- **Danger pattern:** 伤官见官 (Output meets Authority) = disaster for career, health, relationships
- **Key trait:** Hates repetition, seeks efficiency and automation

### 比肩 (Sibling) - Friends, Competition, Self-reliance
- **Positive:** Independent, self-reliant, competitive, good friend
- **Negative:** Stubborn, competitive to fault, self-centered, financial disputes with friends
- **Wealth impact:** Competes for wealth. Many Siblings = wealth gets divided.

### 劫财 (Robbery Wealth) - Partnership, Shared Resources
- **Positive:** Good at teamwork, charismatic leader, brave, action-oriented
- **Negative:** Financial loss through friends, theft tendency, impulsive spending
- **Wealth impact:** Directly takes wealth. Strong Robbery = financial instability unless controlled.

## Wealth Analysis Framework

The relationship between body strength and wealth determines wealth potential:

| Body Strength | Wealth Strength | Wealth Pattern | Strategy |
|-------------|----------------|---------------|----------|
| Strong (身强) | Strong (财旺) | Ideal - can earn and hold | Bold entrepreneurship |
| Strong (身强) | Weak (财弱) | Need to activate wealth sources | Find wealth opportunities |
| Weak (身弱) | Strong (财旺) | Dangerous - wealth brings pressure | Partner with strong people, take minority shares |
| Weak (身弱) | Weak (财弱) | Poor - hard to accumulate | Build skills first, seek stable income |

**Wealth accumulation modes:**
- **Step pattern (阶梯式):** Wealth grows in steps when useful god years arrive
- **Partnership model:** Weak body should always partner with strong body people
- **Industry selection:** Match industry element to useful god element

## Marriage Analysis

**Male:**
- 正财 (Proper Wealth) = Wife (primary)
- 偏财 (Partial Wealth) = Girlfriend/secondary relationship
- Day branch (日支) = Marriage palace (夫妻宫)
- Wealth star heavily attacked or missing = marriage difficulties

**Female:**
- 正官 (Proper Authority) = Husband (primary)
- 七杀 (Seven Killings) = Boyfriend/secondary relationship
- Day branch (日支) = Marriage palace
- Authority star heavily attacked or missing = relationship difficulties

**Marriage timing indicators:**
- Annual/Luck branch combines with day branch (合日支)
- Annual/Luck stem is wealth/authority star
- Marriage palace is activated by combination or clash
- Year stem is proper wealth (male) or proper authority (female)

## Body Strength Quick Reference

### Strong Body Indicators
- Born in season (当令) or supported by season
- Roots in multiple branches (根多地实)
- Heavenly stems provide support
- Limited output/wealth consumption

### Weak Body Indicators
- Born out of season (失令)
- No roots or only distant roots
- Heavy output (食伤) draining energy
- Heavy wealth (财) consuming energy
- Heavy authority (官杀) suppressing energy

### Critical Misjudgment Traps
- Month branch appears supportive but hidden stem is hostile
- Double Output (伤官双透) can drain body completely even in "strong" season
- Double Seven Killings (七杀双现) can suppress body even with apparent support
- Always verify with user's actual life experience
